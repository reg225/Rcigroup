ANDEX B51

Andex B51 is a liquid waterproofing and protective coating for concrete structures based on a hybrid polyurethane. The polyurethane is modifies with specially selected polymers to form a tough, flexible and durable coating.
  Primer and bonding agent with water repellrnt effect plaster boars and plaster render to received wall, floor and concrete reders.

Fields of Application
  • waterproofing of wet areas like bathrooms, toilets, pubic showers & kitchen.
  • waterproofing of roofs, terraces, balconies, domes, aluminium sandwich panels and corrugated sheets.
Storage

Store on pallets and covered in a cool, shaded & dry place. Do not stack.
