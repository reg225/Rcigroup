ABOUT RCI

Rechimar Chemical Ind Ltd(RCI), was founded in 2008 by Okorie Reginald Chimazie. He is from Imo state Umuozo Nwagele LGA, Nigerial. RCI is generally acknowledge to be the best manufacturer supplier of any building material for any building project. As the company earned its deserved reputation by hard work, achieving high customer statisfaction, top-notch Quality and schedule targets, the size of the company and scope of work increased dramatically.

At Rechimar Chemical Ind Ltd, our success is based on that of our customers and we always put our customer first. We are so confident in our products and our service that we adhere to the principle of all profit to our customer & all loss to us and would rather lose our profit than lose a customer.
