B12 Screeding Plaster

Top chioce Screeding Plaster B1 for external or internal use for wall, Mosaie, Marble and Floors..
   It cures to form a tough flexible coating for Screeding and filling cracks on wall. Top Chioce B12 screeding plaster is mixed with Andex B51 to give an excellent waterproofing coating effect when screeding your wall

Fields of Application
  • High quality waterproofing properties when mixed with Andex B51
  • To fill Gaps between Tiles, Marbles
  • Tough flexible coat for screeding of walls
Storage

Store in a dry, cool and shaded area. Protect from sunlight, frost and high humidity.
