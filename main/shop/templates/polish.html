Tile Polish

Using RCI Tile Polish is more than just adding shine to your tile floors. That layer of polish adds protection as well. Polishing floors helps restore a floor's protective finish while filling in any microscopic scratches. When it comes to maintaining beautiful tile floors, RCI tile polish should be added as a necessary part of your long-term maintenanace. Polish the floors every 2-4 months or longer depending on foot traffic.

Fields of Application
  • Measure your floor to ensure that you have enough polish
  • It helps protect your tile floor by restoring its beautiful looks
Storage

Store in a dry, cool and shaded area. Protect from sunlight.
